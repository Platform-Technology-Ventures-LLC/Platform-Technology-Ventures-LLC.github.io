Silicon Workers

Our goal is to integrate AI Agents into our daily workflow, so think of AI agents as your employees who need guidance and management.

Along these lines, while working together, we typically will "task out" employees by making email requests. "Hey Joe, can you look at this data and figure out what the trends are?"

Combinging these into a product offering, we have created an "AI datascientist" that you can email assignments to. From Simple things as merging csv files to parsing old and annoying formats!

NSF-Funded Research

Advancing Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs)

Our SBIR Phase I project focuses on developing DAOs as innovative digital collaboration and governance systems. These blockchain-based structures enable democratic decision-making without centralized authority, opening new possibilities for business collaborations among diverse entities.

Key aspects of our research:

  • Exploring DAOs in voluntary carbon credits, a market projected to reach $40B by 2030
  • Developing a versatile DAO platform for various markets requiring decentralized participation
  • Enhancing operational efficiency and fostering the growth of new technologies
  • Creating more democratic and inclusive systems across multiple sectors
  • Our research aims to revolutionize fields such as finance, healthcare, environmental sustainability, and more
